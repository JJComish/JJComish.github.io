Welcome! This is our remote HCI Experiment for SEng 411 at the University of Victoria.
In this experiment, several numbers will be randomly generate by text or bar chart from 0 to 99 on the 400x400 screen (currently outlined with the black X below).
Please select the largest number/ the longest bar as soon as you see the display. On correct selections the experiment will automatically generate a new group of numbers/bars. There is a total of 40 trials in our experiment（text:20 & bar:20）and should take less than 5 minutes. When you see "Here is the end of the experiment" that means this experiment is done.

Note: Your responses are automatically sent to a Google Sheet. All responses are annomyous and tracked only with a random UUID generated for each run of the experiment. The use of browser extensions such as Privacy Badger may stop your data from being sent.

Before starting the experiment, please read the "Process" carefully below.
Process:
1. Press the "Start Experiment!" button.
2. A series of values will be displayed below. (incorrect selections will be recorded but the experiment will only advance when the largest value is selected)
3. Select the largest value displayed.
4. Continue selecting the largest value until the experiment is finished.
